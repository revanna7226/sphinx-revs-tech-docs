# HR questions

1. Please describe your current role and project.
1. What are the biggest challenges you are facing in the current project?
1. Please describe the most interesting work you've done so far.
1. Please describe your ideal project.
1. How do you keep up with the new technologies and trends in programming?
1. What programming related books did you read?
1. What is the most useful programming book you read so far?
1. Do you work on any personal projects outside work?
1. Have you contributed to an open source project?
1. What is your favourite programming language or framework and why?
1. Did you provide coaching and mentoring for others in the team?
1. How do you share knowledge in our team?
